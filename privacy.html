Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2026

At Dawn ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User-Generated Content

  • Diary Entries: Text content you create and submit.
  • Mood Status: Your selection of "Happy" or "Sad".
  • Approximate Location: City/country level location for global feed display (not precise GPS).

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Anonymous ID: Random identifier generated for your device to sync preferences.
  • Usage Data: Features accessed and interaction patterns.

1.3 Advertising Data

The free version may display ads. Third-party ad networks may use device identifiers (IDFA) and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt-out via iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the App's functionality.
  • To display your entries locally and on the global feed.
  • To detect and prevent abuse via User ID banning.

3. Data Deletion

  • Hard Delete: You can perform a "Hard Delete" in App Settings to instantly wipe your anonymous ID and all linked posts.
  • Account Deletion: Deleting your account permanently removes your data.

4. Third-Party Services

We may share anonymous data with:

  • Ad Networks: (e.g., Google AdMob) for serving ads.
  • Analytics: To understand App usage.

5. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

We respect your rights to access and delete your data. European and Californian users may exercise their rights by contacting us or using the in-app deletion tools.

6. Children's Privacy

Dawn is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with information, please contact us.

7. Contact Us

If you have privacy questions, please contact:

Email: support@dawnapp.com
