Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2026

At Dawn ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

User-Generated Content: We collect the diary entries (up to 140 words) and the mood status (Happy/Sad) that you voluntarily submit to the app.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous metrics regarding how often the app is opened or which features are used to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide the Service: Your diary entries and mood status are used to populate your personal log.
  • Global Feed: If you choose to share your entry, your text and mood status will be displayed on the public "Global Feed" anonymously. We do not attach your name, email, or location to these public posts.
  • Community Stats: We aggregate mood data (e.g., "4,000 Happy today") to display global mood trends.

3. Anonymity

Dawn is designed to be anonymous. We do not require you to create an account with an email address or phone number to browse. If you post to the Global Feed, your identity remains hidden from other users.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
